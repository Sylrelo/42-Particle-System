Viewing Buffers

You can view the contents of an OpenGL buffer by choosing Views > Buffers and then choosing a bufferâ€”Front Buffer, Back Buffer, Alpha Buffer, Stencil Buffer, or Depth Buffer. Buffers not used by your application appear dimmed. Buffer views are available only when your application pauses at a breakpoint.



After selecting a buffer, its contents appear in a window. Each time your application pauses at a breakpoint, each buffer view updates automatically.
